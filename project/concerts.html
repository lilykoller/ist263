Check Out These Artists in NYC




Remi Wolf
March 9, 2022 at the Music Hall of Williamsburg
Remi Wolf is a former American Idol Contestant from California. The 26-year-old singer-songwriter has eight different discographies and will be opening for Lorde on her tour.

Clairo
March 23rd, 2022 at Webster Hall
Claire Cottrill is an American singer-songwriter who was enrolled in the Bandier Program at Syracuse University while her single Pretty Girl went viral. She is currently on tour for her album Sling, which has songs curated from over quarantine.

Dominic Fike
May 3rd, 2022 at the Bowery Ballroom
Dominic Fike is a Naples, Florida-born singer-songwriter-actor. His most recent appearance in the HBO show, Euphoria, is his breakout acting role, and since he has become an internet heartthrob.




Lorde
April 4th, 2022 at the Radio City Music Hall
Lorde is a New Zealand native singer-songwriter. Her work is characterized as electropop with indie undertones. Her most recent album is Solar Power, an ode to her New Zealand roots.

ROLEMODEL
June 12th, 2022 at Irving Plaza
Tucker Pillsbury is a singer-songwriter from Maine. His upcoming debut album, Rx, features 11 singles and will be released on April 8th.
